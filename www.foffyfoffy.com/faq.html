FAQs

The Event

Is there an age limit?

Anyone is welcome! But for safety we do require that any kids aged 3-6 are accompanied into the inflatables by a parent/guardian/carer. We run a safe event and we have multiple staff working throughout our event to ensure safety and good behavior.However, we do ask parents/ carers to be mindful of the potential hazards associated with allowing their smaller children into the same area with older children.

What do I need to wear to take part?

Non-slip Socks must be worn at all times on the inflatable castles. If you forget to bring a non- slip sock of your own, we have Foffy's Wonderland socks available for purchase at $2 each. There will be a 'shoe zone' to leave shoes before you enter the inflatables castles. Please keep an eye on the weather and dress appropriately. Sun protection and extra refreshments are also recommended items to pack. The inflatables might be a little wet if there has been rain, so we recommend bringing a change of clothes, especially socks.

Can I bring my pets with me?

No pets are allowed inside the inflatables, only registered service animals serving those with physical disabilities are allowed entrance into the event.

Can I bring my own food/drinks?

No food & drinks are allowed inside the inflatables and the amusement activities. We have our food & beverage stalls onsite, you are also welcome to bring along your own food and drinks, but please note that drinks need to have a cap.Â 

Can I take my camera inside the inflatables?

We have no problem with you taking your own camera phone in with you. Just keep it safe and secure though as these have been known to fly out the pockets of over enthusiastic bouncers!

Is there a time limit for my visit?

We recommend spending 2-3 hours at the event, but you can choose the duration that feels most comfortable for you.

Will the event still running at a raining day?

In cases of extreme weather, such as heavy rain or tornadoes, we will confirm one day in advance whether we will be open. If your ticket is for that day, you can choose to either get a refund or reschedule.

Is there parking available?

Of course, the parking lot is located across from the event venue. Upon arrival, you will see the parking lot signs. The parking lot has enough space for 1,500 vehicles. Parking is paid and managed by Downsview Park. The fee is $15 per vehicle (on Jun 29, Jun 30, Aug 2, Aug 3, Aug 4, the fee is $25). VIPs have a dedicated parking area closer to the park. VIP ticket holders can park in this area, but spaces are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis.

What if I lost something on the site?

We highly recommend that you take good care of your personal belongings. However, if you do lose something, there is a lost&found area at the gift shop. You can also email our customer service, we will do our best to help you find your lost item, but we cannot guarantee it. Customer service: customer@foffyfoffy.com

Are there any measures in place to protect against mosquitoes in the park at evening time?

Sure, we perform mosquito control every day, but during the summer, mosquitoes might still be present on the grass. We recommend that you also prepare for mosquito protection yourself.
